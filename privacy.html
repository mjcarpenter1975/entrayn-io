1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

Personal information you disclose to us

In Short: We collect personal information that you provide to us.

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the Services, express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products and Services, when you participate in activities on the Services, or otherwise when you contact us.

Personal Information Provided by You. The personal information we collect may include the following:
  • names
  • phone numbers
  • email addresses
  • usernames
  • passwords
  • contact preferences
  • contact or authentication data
  • health and fitness data (training sessions, exercise logs, personal records)
  • recovery and wellness data (daily check-ins, sleep, stress, energy scores)
  • injury and medical history (conditions, movement limitations)
  • GPS and activity data (via Strava integration)
Sensitive Information. When necessary, with your consent or as otherwise permitted by applicable law, we process the following categories of sensitive information:
  • health data
  • biometric data
  • fitness and training data (session logs, exercise performance, personal records)
Apple Health and HealthKit Data. Where you choose to connect Apple Health, we may import health and fitness data including heart rate, HRV, sleep, and activity metrics. Any health and fitness data imported from Apple Health or HealthKit is permanently deleted from Entrayn's servers upon account deletion or disconnection of the Apple Health integration. Health data imported from Apple Health is retained only for as long as your account remains active. Upon account deletion, all imported health data is deleted within 30 days. You can manage or revoke Entrayn's access to Apple Health data at any time through your iPhone's Health app settings under Sources.

Payment Data. We may collect data necessary to process your payment if you choose to make purchases, such as your payment instrument number and the security code associated with your payment instrument. All payment data is handled and stored by Stripe and Apple Pay. You may find their privacy notices at https://stripe.com/privacy and https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/.

Social Media Login Data. We may provide you with the option to register with us using your existing social media account details. If you choose to register in this way, we will collect certain profile information about you from the social media provider, as described in the section called 'HOW DO WE HANDLE YOUR SOCIAL LOGINS?' below.

Application Data. If you use our application(s), we also may collect the following information if you choose to provide us with access or permission:
  • Mobile Device Access. We may request access or permission to certain features from your mobile device, including camera, sensors, storage, social media accounts, health and fitness data (Apple Health / HealthKit integration for heart rate, HRV, sleep and activity data), and other features. Any health and fitness data imported from Apple Health or HealthKit is permanently deleted from Entrayn's servers upon account deletion or disconnection of the Apple Health integration.
  • Mobile Device Data. We automatically collect device information such as your mobile device ID, model, manufacturer, operating system, version information, browser type and version, hardware model, Internet service provider and/or mobile carrier, and Internet Protocol (IP) address.
  • Push Notifications. We may request to send you push notifications regarding your account or certain features of the application(s). If you wish to opt out, you may turn them off in your device's settings.
All personal information that you provide to us must be true, complete, and accurate, and you must notify us of any changes to such personal information.

13. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES

At this stage, no uniform technology standard for recognising and implementing DNT signals has been finalised. As such, we do not currently respond to DNT browser signals or any other mechanism that automatically communicates your choice not to be tracked online.

Why Entrayn exists

"Built by someone who has been the client — through injury, through rehab, through the gap between what a good PT knows and what their software shows them."

I was training for a 50km-a-day charity challenge — 50 consecutive days, raising $50,000 for the Mirabel Foundation supporting vulnerable children. Running 17 hours a week, I dropped strength training to manage the load. I thought I was being efficient. I was wrong. A pinched lateral plantar nerve put me off running for eight months. Saline hydrodissection. Six months of rehab.

The whole time, I was logging in a Google Sheet. My PT had no real visibility. And the gap between what a skilled trainer knows about their client and what their software actually surfaces to them was the most obvious problem I'd ever seen go unsolved.

Entrayn is the tool I wished existed. Not a scheduling app. Not another workout logger. The intelligence layer that closes the gap — so PTs can coach with context, and clients can train with a plan that actually learns from them.

Common questions

FAQ

Does Entrayn replace my job as a PT?+
No — and that's the whole point. PTs got into this because they love coaching people, not building spreadsheets. Entrayn handles programme design and data synthesis so you can spend your time on what actually matters: the relationship, the accountability, the coaching moments. The AI is the engine. You're the expert and the human. Together you deliver better outcomes than either could alone.
Is Entrayn free for my clients?+
Yes. Clients connected to a PT on Entrayn get the full premium client app at no cost to them — it's included in your PT subscription. Solo clients can use a free tier, or upgrade to premium for $9.99/month.
What happens when I join the waitlist?+
You'll receive a confirmation and be among the first to access Entrayn when we launch. Early access members are offered a locked-in price before we move to standard pricing.
How does the AI programme builder work?+
Describe your client in plain English — their goal, training age, available days, equipment, any injuries — and Entrayn generates a complete, structured training block in seconds. You review it, adjust anything you like, and publish it to the client. As the client logs sessions and check-ins, the AI refines weight suggestions, rest day guidance, and coaching insights block by block.
What makes Entrayn different from Everfit or TrueCoach?+
Most PT platforms charge per client, so the more you grow the more you pay. Entrayn is flat-rate — unlimited clients, one price. But the deeper difference is the data flywheel: Entrayn gets more valuable the longer a client trains on it. Their accumulated session history, check-in data, and progress trends make every subsequent programme smarter. No other platform does this.
Is my data secure?+
Yes. Entrayn is built on Supabase with row-level security — each trainer and client only ever sees their own data. Your client data is never used to train third-party AI models.

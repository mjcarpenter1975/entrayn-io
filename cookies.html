What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, such as your login state or preferences, to make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

How we use cookies

Entrayn uses a minimal set of cookies to operate the platform. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
sb-access-token Essential Keeps you logged in to your Entrayn account (Supabase authentication) Session
sb-refresh-token Essential Refreshes your login session automatically (Supabase authentication) 30 days
__stripe_mid Functional Fraud prevention for payment processing (Stripe) 1 year
__stripe_sid Functional Session identifier for payment processing (Stripe) 30 minutes

Essential cookies

Essential cookies are required for the platform to function. They cannot be disabled without preventing you from logging in or using core features of Entrayn. No consent is required for essential cookies under Australian privacy law.

Third-party cookies

Stripe, our payment processor, sets cookies for fraud prevention and session management during checkout. These are functional cookies necessary to process payments securely. Stripe's cookie policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from logging in to Entrayn. Instructions for managing cookies in common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
